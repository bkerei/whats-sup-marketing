Welcome to Sup!

The Android App designed to help the homeless and displaced of Wellington find services to help.

Download Now

It's User-Friendly

Just open Sup! and you're greeted by an animated tutorial that explains how it works

It Finds You

As a location based app, it pings your phone so you can figure out where you're heading faster

It's Informative

We give you the address, number, opening hours and a bit about the service plus a helpful picture of the front of the building

It's Designed to Help

Sup! is an App created with love to help anyone in the Wellington Community who needs help to improve their circumstances
